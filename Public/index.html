ONGC feedback form

The feedback form I developed for ONGC (Oil and Natural Gas Corporation) was designed to gather comprehensive feedback on various services provided. It included fields for asNode.js catering services such as meal quality, timeliness, staff efficiency, and overall satisfaction ratings. The form ensured user-friendly interaction with clear labels and responsive design, allowing easy submission and processing of feedback data. It was integrated with a Node.js backend and MySQL database for secure and efficient data management and collection.
